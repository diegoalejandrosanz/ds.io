Music Industry

The main interest for this project was to understand why one of our frontline artists secured the most consumed artist in the world within that year and how this was translated to revenue for the company.

Alongside, we discovered potential strategies used during the year that help us to segment the new era of digitalization of the music industry and how to replicate to more artists within our label.

We first recognized all consumption year over year of the industry at that moment and translated to percentages, looked through the ups and downs in consumption and focus in the specific segment we were interested.

A full year behavior of consumption compared to other top aritsts showed us the spikes and the strength of the strategy applied, letting us know that on a single day the artist was streamed 73.5% from total global streaming that day.

The analysis (MoM) helped us to determine growth rates, results were that streams on the month of April grew +514% vs. prior month and listeners +24% and by this means fans were constinously playing the album just released.

The engagement was impressive, however most of the streaming was coming from the core fans of the artist and in a lower level new engaged audience this was a key takeaway to improve results and turnover into similar strategy approaches.

Citibikes in NYC

Main interest for this project was understand which Citibikes stations around NYC were the most used, and what were the most trafficked routes.

In addition, we identified by tiers of age customers who used Citi's service the most and how to improve from that starting point strategies to punctual audiences.

Promptly recognized all the stations and the top starting point station for users of the service. 46.7% of all rides strarted from Pershing Square North Station.

According to this information we had the opportunity to improve times of re-load on bikes for that specific station. Decreasing user complaints.

Testing visual promts for presentations is a top priority, this may help to the audience to understand in a better way what it's being presented, Therefore we showed 3 avail options and how we could spot the most concurred stations.

The first bullet on the analysis showed the type of customer where 16.8% were subscriber users type, 37.4% non subscribers, meaning that they have to fill all the info every time they take a ride and 45.3% undisclosed this information .

From all the customers that use the service 17.9% are men 17.7% are women a very close ratio, 32.69% marked as unknown and 60.8% did not described it.

Finally we wanted to know what demographic was the CitiBikes user primarily, finding that an outstanding 17% of the users were 50 years old and up while 22.4% were 20 years old or less, meaning 60.6% were in between ages of 20 and 50 years .
